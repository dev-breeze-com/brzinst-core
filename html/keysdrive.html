Breeze::OS Installer -- Crypto Keys USB Drive

You must select a drive, from the list on the left,
onto which to save the crypto keys.
The USB drive will be partitioned and formatted.
Ensure that the right drive is selected; and,
any data on it, is unneeded.

If you have not yet inserted a USB drive; then, insert one now, and
click on reload.

Select a USB drive before Continuing.
