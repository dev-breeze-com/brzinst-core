Breeze::OS Installer -- Drive Formatting

Drive: %selected-drive%
Model: %selected-drive-model%
If you format your partitions, all data on them will be lost.

You must select the filesystem for each partition you wish to reformat.
You must also select the location for the boot block;
the Master Boot Record is selected by default.
You may select a format with bad block checking.

You may use the buttons, below, to customize you partitioning.

Click on below to Repartition your drive.
Click on below to Restore your partitions.
Click on below to Format your partitions.
Click on to Continue without formatting.
