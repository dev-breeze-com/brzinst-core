Breeze::OS Installer -- Localization

Localization consists in configuring your computer according to the following settings:

  • Locale for language of use setting.
  • Timezone for date and time settings.
  • Keyboard for type of keyboard to use.
  • Layout for keyboard layout (e.g. for french, etc.).
  • Keymap for mapping keystroke to characters.
The US locale and UTF-8 character encoding are used by default.
