Breeze::OS Installer -- Network Installation

If you only require a desktop system, then
you should pick standalone as a workgroup installation.
Otherwise, either pick network-client or network-master,
depending on, whether or not,
you want a client or master desktop computer.
If you want a network-client,
you may wish to replicate the master computer's desktop
installation by selecting network for the source of packages.
If your selection is standalone, then each computer,
even though networked, will not share passwords.
The master/client client installation uses
the Network Information Service (NIS) to share passwords.
The Kodiak©®, Katmai©®, and Mach©® releases of our distro
will offer the SALT network service, part of the Clubs setup,
as an alternative to NIS, for sharing passwords.


Select a workgroup type, or click on to Continue.
