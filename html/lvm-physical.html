Breeze::OS Installer -- LVM Volumes

A physical volume (PV) can be created using any block device.
A logical volume is allocated from a volume group and is analogous to a disk partition.
Our partitioning schemes create one volume group per mount point.
You must select the partitions you wish to encrypt if encryption was selected.
You can either create, merge, or ignore a physical volume.
