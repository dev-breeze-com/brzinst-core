Breeze::OS Installer -- DHCP setup

The possible network setups are:

  • loopback your computer is not connected,
  • static your computer is on a Local Area Network,
  • dhcp the network is setup using DHCP (e.g. Cable/DSL modem)
The following information is required:
  • master the name of the LAN master computer, if any.
  • domain the Internet domain name of your LAN.
  • IP address the Internet address of your master computer.
  • gateway the internet address of your gateway computer;
  • nameserver the nameserver to use for DHCP queries.
Your gateway computer is most probably your master computer.
The Dynamic Host Configuration Protocol (DHCP) automatically setups the
Internet Protocol (IP) addresses required by your computer host,
to enable IP connections.
If your are using DHCP, then the nameserver and gateway entries
are automatically retrieved; otherwise, you must enter the appropriate
information; which is provided by your Internet Service Provider (ISP).
If you are on a Local Area Network (LAN); then, you must provide
the required information on the left, to connect your computer.
Your ISP DHCP hostname may differ from your computer hostname.
Some ISPs provide their own DHCP hostname; enter it
in the appropriate field on the left.
