Breeze::OS Installer -- User Account Creation

User accounts are required.
You must have an account for yourself, and
for the system administrator [root], and the security manager [secadmin].
The sysadmin or root acccount, as well as,
the secadmin or secadmin account, are automatically created.
The default password for both the root and secadmin account, is brzadmin.
You should choose to change the password for root and secadmin,
by filling the password entries on the left, and clicking on the apply button.
To create your user account and other optional ones,
you must fill-in the entry fields on the left.
Only your username, full name, and password are necessary.
Your password is used to login on your computer.

Click on to Save your Settings.
Click on to Continue.
