Breeze::OS Installer -- Locale Selection

Setting your locale will select settings, such as:

  • Message setting for localized messages.
  • Monetary setting for monetary formats.
  • Numeric setting for number formats.
  • Time setting for time and date formats, etc..
The selected settings will be used by your desktop, after rebooting.
The US locale and UTF-8 character encoding are used by default.

Select a locale, or click on to Continue.
