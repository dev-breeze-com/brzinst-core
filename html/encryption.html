Breeze::OS Installer -- Drive Encryption Schemes

The only scheme offered for hard-drive encryption is DM-Crypt or LUKS.
LUKS provides block device encryption, and is used to encrypt your entire drive.
One keyfile is used for all partitions, and your hard-drive is erased before encryption.
The expertise of an average or expert user is needed to select one keyfile per partition.
You must enter a password for decryption before continuing.
