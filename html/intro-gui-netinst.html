Welcome to the Breeze:OS©® Linux/BSD Distribution

This is the Network Install version of our distribution.
All packages are downloaded from the Internet by default.
But, you can also use this version for installation from your LAN.
All available desktops are offered, such as the Xfce, KDE, and Mate desktop.
The Libre Office or Open Office suite is also offered.
The installation of this release is straightforward.
The installation process goes according to the steps below.
You must have a 10+ Gig hard-drive; and 512M, or more, of real memory.
Your computer must be network/internet enabled.
We also recommend that the installation be done on a separate drive.
Other releases, to come, are the Kodiak desktop, the Katmai server,
and the Mach developer editions.

The following steps are taken in the installation process:

  • hard-drive a hard-drive must be selected.
  • scheme a partitioning scheme must be selected.
  • crypto an encryption scheme may be selected.
  • partition automatic or custom partitioning is used.
  • format the partitioned drive must be formatted.
  • filesystems the selected ones must be created.
