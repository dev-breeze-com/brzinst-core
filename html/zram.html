Breeze::OS Installer -- Compressed RAM Drive

Compressed RAM drives are used to increase the amount of available
Random Access Memmory (RAM) accessible by your computer.
The appropriate kernel module must first be loaded, i.e. (zram).
The number of processors that are seen as enabled, by the running kernel,
is taken into account. For low memory machines a fourth of the total memory,
as ZRAM space, is usually a good choice.
