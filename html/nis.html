Breeze::OS Installer -- Network Information Service (NIS)

The Network Information Service (NIS) requires the information on the left.
Sharing, between hosts, of password and other various files can then be enabled.
You may enter a different name for your NIS server and NIS domain.
The IP address, you specify, must be that of the host chosen as NIS server.
The NIS domain must consists only of letters and numbers.
The NIS submask specifies the accessible hosts in your NIS domain.
If your NIS server is your master computer; then, use
the same host and domain names as that of your master computer.
