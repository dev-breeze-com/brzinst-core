Breeze::OS Installer -- Internet Connection

To the left is a list of Internet connections from which to choose.

You must have an account with an Internet Service Provider (ISP) if you want to connect to the World Wide Web.

You must select the type of account, that your ISP provides; for example, a dialup or adsl account.

You must provide user-name and password to enable your Internet connection.

Select a connection or account, or click on to Continue.
