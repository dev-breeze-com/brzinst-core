Welcome to the Breeze:OS©® Community Edition Linux Distribution

Our Breeze::OS©® system is a desktop oriented Linux distro and desktop.
The Breeze::OS©® desktop is only included in the Breeze::OS Installer©® release.
The Community Edition includes the Gnome, Kde, Xfce, or Lxde desktop.
It also contains the Libre Office or Open Office office suite.
The installation of this release is straightforward.
The installation process goes according to the steps below.
You must have a 10G hard-drive.
You must have, at least, 256M, or more, of real memory; but.
It is preferable that your computer be network/internet enabled.

The curses based installer provides the following keys for traversal.

  • F2 for the left-most navigation box.
  • F3 for the listview or attributes box.
  • F4 for the drive information box.
  • F5 for the page commands box.
  • F6 for the bottom-most command box.

Other releases, to come, are the Kodiak desktop, the Katmai server,
and the Mach developer editions. The following steps are taken in the installation process:

  • hard-drive a hard-drive must be selected.
  • scheme a partitioning scheme must be selected.
  • crypto an encryption scheme may be selected.
  • partition automatic or custom partitioning is used.
  • format the partitioned drive must be formatted.
  • filesystems the selected ones must be created.
