Breeze::OS Installer -- Keyboard Map Selection

The Qwerty en_US keyboard map is picked by default.
Keyboard maps are used to associate characters to keystrokes. They are able to remap your keystrokes to permit the use of accented characters.
By selecting the entry locale, a keymap is picked; based on the one you selected on the locale page; so that accented characters can be handled.

Select a keymap, or click on to Continue.
