Breeze::OS Installer -- Internet Connection

The list of Internet connections is displayed on the left.

You must have an account with an Internet Service Provider (ISP) if you want to connect to the World Wide Web.

You must select the type of account, that your ISP provides;
for example, a dialup or adsl account.

To enable your Internet connection,
you must provide a user-name and password.

You must first select a connection or account.

Click on to Save your Settings, or on to Continue.
