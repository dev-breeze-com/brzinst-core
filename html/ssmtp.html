Breeze::OS Installer -- sSMTP Configuration

sSMTP is a very simple MTA to get mail off the system
to a mailhub. It is used to automatically send bug reports
to the Breeze::OS developer website.
You must specify the recipient (e.g. username@mail.com);
and, the mailhub to be used (e.g. smtp.gmail.com');
If TLS Encryption is used; you must provide
the mail account username and password.

Click on to Save your sSMTP Settings.
