Breeze::OS Installer -- Save Crypto Keys

You must now choose, whether or not, to encrypt your USB flash drive.
If yes, then you must select the encryption parameters on the left.
The parameters are used in the encryption of the crypto keys container.
You must also provide a password.
The USB drive will be partitioned and formatted with EFI and Ext4.
The container is the Ext4 partition, and is to be mounted as /mnt/lukskeys.

Click on to Save your Crypto Keys.
Click on to Continue.
