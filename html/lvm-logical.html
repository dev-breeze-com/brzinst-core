Breeze::OS Installer -- LVM Logical Volumes

A logical volume is allocated from a volume group and is analogous to a disk partition.
By default, logical volumes are created according to the selected partitioning scheme.
The total allocated size must not exceed the total space for logical volumes.
The total allocated space for logical volumes is %lvm-footprint% MB.
You may modify the size settings of the logical volumes below.
