Breeze::OS Installer -- Network Setup

The possible network setups are:

  • loopback your computer is not connected.
  • static your computer is on a Local Area Network.
  • dhcp your computer network is setup using DHCP.
The following information is required:
  • master the name of the LAN master computer, if any.
  • domain the Internet domain name of your LAN.
  • IP address the Internet address of your master computer.
  • gateway the internet address of your gateway computer.
  • nameserver the nameserver to use for DHCP queries.
The Dynamic Host Configuration Protocol (DHCP)
DHCP automatically setups the Internet Protocol (IP) addresses required by
your computer host, to enable IP connections. Your gateway computer is
probably your master computer.
If your are using DHCP, then the nameserver and gateway entries are
automatically retrieved; otherwise, you must enter the appropriate information;
which is provided by your Internet Service Provider (ISP).
If you are on a Local Area Network (LAN); then, you must provide
the required information on the left, to connect your computer.
Your Internet Service Provider's DHCP hostname may differ from
your computer hostname. Some ISPs provide their own DHCP hostname;
enter it in the appropriate field on the left.
