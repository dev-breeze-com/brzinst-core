Breeze::OS Installer -- Internet Connection

The list of Internet connections is displayed on the left.
You must have an account with an Internet Service Provider (ISP) if you want to connect to the World Wide Web.
You must select the type of account, that your ISP provides; i.e. a dialup, wireless, cable, or adsl account.
If you do not have a self-managed router modem; then to enable
your Internet connection, you will have to provide
a user-name and password.

Select a connection type, or click on to Continue.
