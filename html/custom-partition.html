Breeze::OS Installer -- Hard Drive Selection

If you have Windows partitions,
you should reserve half of the space, on the selected drive, for your Windows files.

You must make sure, therefore, that files kept in your Windows partitions do not use more than
half of the disk space.

If you reformat your drives; you will loose all your data.
You cannot recover data, from reformatted drives.

Select one of the hard-drives listed on the left !
